Exercise
Lunch walk every weekday — 45 mins, brisk pace. Your foundation.
Steps: Start 8,000/day, build to 12,000 over 6–8 weeks.
Weekends: Active family time — parks, pram walks. Counts.
Home workout 2x/week (20 mins): 3 rounds — 10 squats → 8 push-ups → 20s plank → 10 glute bridges.
🏃
Morning Jog (2x/week)
Nursery drop-off days — home by 8am, 30 mins out.
Weeks 1–2: 1 min jog / 2 min walk × 25–30 mins
Weeks 3–4: 2 min jog / 1 min walk × 25–30 mins
Weeks 5–6: 5 min jog / 1 min walk, repeat
Weeks 7–8: 10–15 min continuous jog, build from there
Always do 5 mins mobility before jogging.
